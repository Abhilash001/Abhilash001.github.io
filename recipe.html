Recepie for Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish made in India that usually consists of rice along with various spices and chicken or mutton in case of non-vegetarian dishes.

Ingredients required:

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • 1 tbsp. ginger garlic paste
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 3 green chilies slit (adjust to suit your taste)
  • ½ cup + 1 tbsp. yogurt / curd
  • 2 tbsps. Lemon juice (adjust)
  • ¼ tsp green cardamom powder
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Instructions:

  1. Soak rice for at least 20 to 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken using ingredients under marination. Set this aside.
  3. Add oil to a pressure cooker and heat it. Add the dry spices.
  4. Add onions and fry till they turn golden.
  5. Add chicken and fry till the color changes.
  6. Drain off the rice completely and add it to the cooker.
  7. Cook till the water very little is left, cover the pressure cooker lid and turn on the flame to very low.
  8. Let the pressure go down. Fluff up the rice with a fork. Or transfer it immediately to a wide utensil to prevent turning mushy.
  9. Sprinkle chopped coriander leaves. Serve with onion and cucumber salad. And you're done!
